General Information

General

This year, we’ll host a series of sweetlife experiences around the country that will capture the essence of the sweetlife — music, food and community — in a more intimate environment. We feel this is the best way to build local communities and to honor the legacy of music that has been with us since the beginning.

Food

SweetLife Block Party features more delicious food than you could eat in a single weekend, with over 100 vendors serving everything from wood-fired pizza and hand-crafted burgers to authentic street tacos and fresh-from-the-wok pad thai. With plenty of options for every taste and budget, and ample menu items for vegetarian, vegan and gluten-free guests, we promise you’ll never go hungry.

Drink

We offer curated specialty cocktails from some of the country’s most respected bar programs as well as a large Craft Beer selection. You can find beer gardens throughout the festival grounds serving craft on draft, mixed cocktails, and cold beer.
